* Clojure的正则表达式支持 [[使用修饰符flag进行高级搜索]]，但是语法不太一样
** 以(?<flag>)开头的Clojure regex字面量会设置修饰符，例如模式#"(?i)yo"会匹配字符串"yo", "yO", "Yo"和 "YO"
* re-seq函数是Clojure regex的苦力
** #+BEGIN_SRC clojure
(re-seq #"\w+" "one-two/three")
;=> ("one" "two" "three")

(re-seq #"\w*(\w)" "one-two/three")
;=> (["one" "e"] ["two" "o"] ["three" "e"])
#+END_SRC
